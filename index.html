About Me

As a soon-to-be University of North Texas graduate, I'm excited to finally put my years of study to good use. Technical communication is a broad profession and I look forward to carving out my own niche of expertise over time. Furthermore, my goal is to be able to blend my own creativity with the development processes of industry products in this high-tech world.

Something I find really interesting about tech comm is how often I see older methodologies blended or updated with current practices. Tech comm is a relatively new field of expertise, yet one can trace its roots as far back as we've had written documentation.
